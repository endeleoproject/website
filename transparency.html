Transparency

The Endeleo Project works hard to ensure that your gift is put to good use, honoring your investment by minimizing overhead costs and keeping an "open book" policy - meaning we let you see exactly how your money is being put to work. We want your gift to go where it is needed most, and we want you to know it.

"When I give to Endeleo, I know exactly how my money is being invested. There is so little waste and it's making it to the right place."
