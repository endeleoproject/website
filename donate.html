Make a Donation

We take pride in having exceptionally low administrative costs to ensure that as much of your donation supports progress as possible (administrative costs so far are just over 3%).

Because The Endeleo Project is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization, your donation is tax deductible. When you make a donation, we will send you a receipt for your donation either by mail or e-mail that you can use to take a deduction on your federal income taxes.

Donations can be made by sending a paper check payable to "The Endeleo Project" to the address below or using a credit card through Google Checkout (thanks to our Google Grant, there is no charge for using your credit card). For those people donating via paper check, your receipt will be mailed via regular mail unless you send us an email address (please note that email is preferable as it is less likely to be lost in the mail, it allows us to serve you more quickly and it lowers our administrative costs).

Please note that there are no minimum donations. We are happy to get donations of any size. Finally, please note that Endeleo reserves the right to make final decisions as to the best allocation of funds.
